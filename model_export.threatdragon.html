Top 8 Prioritized Risks

  1. OTP brute-force account takeover – Automated OTP guessing on Flipkart's mobile login (4-6 digit OTP)
  2. IDOR on order/address endpoints – Sequential IDs allow cross-user data access (DPDP Act violation)
  3. Price/coupon tampering at checkout – Client-side manipulation of cart totals during Big Billion Days
  4. Razorpay/PayU webhook spoofing – Forged payment success callbacks cause unpaid order shipments
  5. Flash sale DDoS (Big Billion Days) – Bot/attacker traffic floods crash checkout at 10x normal load
  6. Mass user PII exposure – 400M+ user records (phone, address, Aadhaar) at risk of breach
  7. Fake seller registration and listing fraud – Fraudulent sellers with stolen KYC collect payments without shipping
  8. Admin account compromise – Phished admin credentials grant access to payouts, catalog, and user data
